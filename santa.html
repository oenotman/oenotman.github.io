Santa, aka: The Saint

I have had Santa for 6 years! He is a barn favorite because of his great manners.

Santa and I have gone to competitions in 4 states. Unfortunately, he got hurt which caused him to retire early. Santa now lives in Kentucky, and is enjoying retirement there. I visit him in the summer. Santa is from Europe. I got him in December just before Christmas.

A bit about Santa.

Santa is a 17 year old European Warmblood gelding. He has a beautiful grey coat. We used to compete in the jumper and equitation ring together. Santa loves carrots. It's his favorite treat! I make sure to bring him bunches of those when I visit.
